Cat stamp

Materials: Black ink, blue watercolor.

Subject matter: Sphynx cat.

This is a block carved print made by carving grooves into stamp material then putting ink on the stamp and pressing it onto paper.

This peice was put on display for a showing at The Lawrence House Centre For Arts in Sarnia, Ontario.
